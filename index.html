Welcome

I am an accomplished team leader and plant breeding scientist with over a decade of experience managing R&D initiatives in small grains, legumes, and clonally propagated aromatic crops. I thrive in collaborative environments, overseeing projects from conception to completion and aligning them with strategic goals.

My work combines strategic planning, technical innovation, and mentorship. I’ve built high-performing teams, secured funding for complex research programs, and delivered measurable outcomes in plant variety development and functional genomics.

With expertise ranging from molecular genetics to large-scale field trials, I focus on advancing agricultural science, developing improved crop varieties, and fostering innovation that benefits both industry and academia.

Research Highlights

Led multi-year plant breeding programs delivering new varieties of mint and chickpea now cultivated across thousands of acres.

Directed genomic discovery initiatives identifying genes controlling flowering time, vernalization, and quality traits in crops.

Built and mentored high-performing, cross-functional teams driving innovation in plant science and applied research.
